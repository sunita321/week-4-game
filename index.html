Crystals Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. Each crystal has a different value. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

To win you need to match your total score to the random number to guess. You lose if your total score goes above the random number to guess.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
